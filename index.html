Common Questions

Everything you need to know about your watch membership

What happens if I damage a watch?

All watches come with insurance coverage for normal wear and tear. For accidental damage, there's a small deductible based on the watch's value. We're reasonable and understand accidents happen.

Can I select specific watches or is it random?

You have complete control over which watches you select. Browse our full collection and pick exactly what matches your style. Our style advisors can also recommend watches that complement your wardrobe based on your preferences.

Are these brand new watches?

All watches arrive in like-new condition. We professionally service, polish, and sterilize each timepiece between users. Most cannot tell the difference from brand new, and all mechanical components are certified to function perfectly.

How does the watch ownership work for 12-month plans?

After completing a full 12-month membership, you can select one of the watches you've experienced to keep permanently. This timepiece becomes yours to own forever—a premium watch that would normally cost ₹10,000-15,000 at retail.

What happens if I want to cancel?

After your initial commitment period (6 or 12 months), you can cancel anytime with 30 days' notice. Simply return your current watches and your membership will be closed with no further charges.
